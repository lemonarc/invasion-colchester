Meet Your Heroes

There will be characters from Star Wars, Ghostbusters, Doctor Who, Predators, Jurassic Park, Movie Princesses and more, as well as your favourite Comic Book and Movie Superheroes, plus Show Cars from TV and film!

You can see amazing live performances from some supremely talented local musicians.

There will also be a number of fun competitions you can take part in with a great selection of prizes to be won!

From Fenwick to Ace Comics, Lion Walk and Culver Square, all of this is organised to encourage our fabulous supporters to give some money to our charities...
St Helena Hospice, Colchester & Ipswich Hospitals Charity, and Mind (Mid & North East Essex)
...who provide essential services to the community.

Come and join in the fun!

Fancy Dress Competition

Love dressing up? Under 14? Then this one’s for you. Step into Culver Square Shopping Centre on Saturday 6 September for the Invasion Colchester Children’s Fancy Dress Competition!

Age Categories

There will be three different age catergories... 4 and under, 5-8 and 9+ ...and 1st, 2nd and 3rd placed prizes in each category.

How to Enter

Rock your best costume—store-bought or homemade—head to Colchester city centre for Invasion Colchester, and be by the judging area in Culver Square at the allotted time.

On the Day

The competition zone will be roped off. Only entrants inside, please. (Parents of very little ones can accompany them; otherwise, we ask grown-ups to stay behind the rope to avoid crowding.)

Winners announced, prizes awarded… then a group photo to cap it off.

Good luck!

Show Cars

Batmobiles. Transformers. Ecto-1. Mr Bean’s Mini. The Back to the Future DeLorean. Screen-famous wheels roll into Colchester every year—and this year’s lineup is just as epic.
